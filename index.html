Mapping Pakistan's Evolving Education Landscape

As a developing country, Pakistan has been subject to radical economic, social, and demographic shifts. The education sector in particular has been changing rapidly as parts of the country undergo aggressive development. The exploration and analysis of these changes is made easy by the Developyst data-platform which harnesses all available statistical data to present the knowledge hidden within it.
